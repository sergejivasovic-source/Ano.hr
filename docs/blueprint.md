# **App Name**: ANO InsureConnect

## Core Features:

- Informative Content Display: Present structured information on services, insurance types, and company values using cards, accordions, and grids, with clear differentiation for core messages.
- Multi-language Interface: Provide a user interface that supports switching between Croatian, English, Slovenian, and Bosnian/Serbian content to cater to diverse business clients.
- Client Inquiry Form: Enable prospective clients to submit detailed inquiries via a contact form, capturing necessary information such as name, company, email, and specific message.
- Damage Claim Initiation: Offer a guided workflow or dedicated form for existing clients to submit initial details for damage claims, categorized by insurance type.
- External Client Platform Access: Provide direct links and clear CTAs for existing clients to access the external 'MOJ ANO' platform for managing policies, documents, and claims.
- AI-powered Insurance Advisor Tool: Implement an interactive AI tool (chatbot or FAQ search) to answer common business insurance questions and explain ANO's services based on structured knowledge.
- SEO-Optimized Content Structure: Develop a semantically robust HTML structure that enhances search engine discoverability and indexing for business insurance-related queries.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A clear, vibrant blue (#1BB8D7), representing professionalism, clarity, and trust in the financial and insurance sectors.
- Background color: A light, desaturated blue (#EEF6F8), creating a clean, expansive feel that complements the primary brand color.
- Accent color: A fresh, light teal (#7CDACD), offering a subtle visual contrast for interactive elements and highlights, aligning with the primary blue hue.
- Headline and body font: 'Roboto' (sans-serif), chosen for its modern readability, professional appearance, and support for Croatian diacritics, ensuring clarity across all content.
- Utilize modern, clean line-art icons that convey business professionalism and specific service areas, maintaining a corporate and trustworthy aesthetic.
- Desktop content max-width set to 1200-1320px for optimal viewing, featuring a hero section in a two-column layout (text/visual) and card grids of 3-4 columns; responsive design adapts layouts for tablet and mobile, including a sticky mobile footer for key service CTAs.
- Implement subtle hover animations on interactive components (buttons, cards) and smooth scrolling for in-page navigation, ensuring a polished and engaging user experience without aggressive motions.
